About Me


After years of contributing to groundbreaking research in condensed matter physics, I am now transitioning into the field of project management.

My experiences as a researcher have equipped me with critical skills such as problem-solving, strategic planning, and effective collaboration across multidisciplinary teams. Building on this foundation, I have pursued certifications and hands-on management roles, overseeing projects and coordinating teams to drive success.

This shift reflects my passion for bridging technical expertise with organizational leadership, enabling me to guide innovative initiatives and deliver impactful results.
